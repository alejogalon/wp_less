Company Overview

MIT graduates with advanced degrees in engineering and expertise in computer vision, image processing and machine learning founded NetVirta in 2013.

The company built its technology for the most discerning customers—doctors demanding medical-grade accuracy—before expanding to serve the needs of apparel and sports equipment manufacturers and retailers.
